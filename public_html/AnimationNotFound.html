About the Game

Animation Not Found was created for the GMTK Game Jam 2025, where the theme was "Loop." The game tells the story of a small sprite who becomes trapped inside a corrupted game world. Due to the corruption, the sprite has been completely stripped of all his animations, leaving him unable to move or interact with the world around him.

Early in his journey, the sprite meets Zippy, a flying zip folder who provides him with his first animation frames: idle and walk cycles. This is where the innovative gameplay begins. Players can open the in-game animation editor, where they drag and arrange frames in the correct sequence to create smooth, functional animations. Once assembled, these animations become usable in the game world.

As players progress through the game, they discover additional animation frames scattered throughout the corrupted world. Each new animation unlocks new abilities and opens up previously inaccessible areas. The game cleverly combines puzzle-solving, platforming, and creative animation design into a unique gameplay experience that challenges players to think like animators while exploring a vibrant pixel art world.
