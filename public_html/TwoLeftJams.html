About the Game

TwoLeftJams was created during an intense 2-day game jam with a group of friends, where we set out to create something truly cooperative. The result was a game that challenges two players to work together to accomplish the seemingly simple task of making a sandwich - but with a twist that makes it hilariously difficult.

In this co-op experience, each player controls just one hand. Everything requires coordination and communication. One player turns on the water tap while the other washes their hand. One holds the jar steady while the other unscrews the lid. Every action, from spreading condiments to stacking ingredients, demands teamwork and timing.

The game culminates with players carefully placing their creation into a box, where they receive a score based on their sandwich-making prowess. What starts as a simple concept quickly becomes a test of cooperation, patience, and coordination. The judges loved it enough to award us first place, and the public vote gave us a strong second place finish out of over 100 participants.
